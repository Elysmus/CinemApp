Introduction

It is no secret that movies are among the most common forms of entertainment and more so they cut across all age groups. Naturally going to the movies is a hobby shared by many, our beloved Kampala being no exception. This however becomes tricky in the case that one wants to view the catalogue and showtimes of their favourite new films because there is no formally established manner of browsing nearby cinema content be it coming soon or already showing and their ticket prices.
One might argue that that isnt a big deal, however things only get worse when movie-goers try to book a ticket without actually going through the inconvenience of travelling to the cinema. This limits one to either watch on-the-spot or go back to running errands and risk missing the opening scenes of the movie. Owing to the above stated problem, there is a need for a more reliable alternative to booking tickets on site.
